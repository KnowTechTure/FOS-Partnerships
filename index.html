1 The organization commits to SRHR, reproductive justice, and intersectional feminist values
1.1 Does the organization promote and address Sexual and Reproductive Health and Rights (SRHR), reproductive justice, and intersectional feminist values?
  • The organization contributes to making a SIGNIFICANT SRHR impact embodying the values of intersectional feminism (centering the voices of those experiencing overlapping forms of oppression), reproductive justice (the human right to maintain personal bodily autonomy), anti-racism (opposing social inferiority of Black people in general and Black women in particlar) and/or anti-ableism (discrimination based on disabilities), in its vision, mission, strategy, programming and commitments
Alignment Criteria
  • FOR ALL ORGANIZATIONS: The organization's vision, purpose, strategy and commitments are focused on making a significant SRHR, intersectional feminist, reproductive justice, anti-racist and anti-ableist impact or laying the groundwork for future SRHR impact; the organization's SRHR aspirations are bold and progressive; the organization's vision, purpose, strategy, and commitments are aligned with Fòs Feminista's values.
